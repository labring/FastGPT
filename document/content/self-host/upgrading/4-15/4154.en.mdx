## 📦 Upgrade Guide

### Configure the required FE_DOMAIN

The FastGPT app now validates `FE_DOMAIN` at startup. Set it to the complete, externally accessible
URL of your FastGPT frontend. Do not use `localhost`. `FE_DOMAIN` is still required when a separate
`FILE_DOMAIN` is configured.

```bash
FE_DOMAIN=https://fastgpt.example.com
```
