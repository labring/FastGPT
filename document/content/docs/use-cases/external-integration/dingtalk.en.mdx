Starting from version 4.8.16, FastGPT commercial version supports direct integration with DingTalk bots without additional APIs.

## 1. Create DingTalk Enterprise Internal App

1. Create an enterprise internal app in the [DingTalk Developer Console](https://open-dev.dingtalk.com/fe/app).

![Image 1](/imgs/dingtalk-bot-1.png)

2. Get the **Client ID** and **Client Secret**.

![Image 2](/imgs/dingtalk-bot-2.png)

## 2. Add Publishing Channel to FastGPT

In FastGPT, select the app you want to integrate. On the **Publishing Channels** page, create a new publishing channel for DingTalk bot integration.

Fill in the **Client ID** and **Client Secret** obtained earlier into the configuration dialog.

![Image 3](/imgs/dingtalk-bot-3.png)

After creation, click the **Request URL** button and copy the callback address.

## 3. Add **Bot** App Capability

In the DingTalk Developer Console, click **Add App Capability** on the left, and add the **Bot** app capability to the enterprise internal app you just created.

![Image 4](/imgs/dingtalk-bot-4.png)

## 4. Configure Bot Callback Address

Click the **Bot** app capability on the left, then set the **Message Receiving Mode** at the bottom to **HTTP Mode**, and fill in the FastGPT callback address copied earlier as the message receiving address.

![Image 5](/imgs/dingtalk-bot-5.png)

After debugging, click **Publish**.

## 5. Publish App
After the bot is published, you still need to publish the app version on the **Version Management and Publishing** page.

![Image 6](/imgs/dingtalk-bot-6.png)

After clicking **Create New Version**, set the version number and description, then click save to publish.

![Image 7](/imgs/dingtalk-bot-7.png)

After the app is published, you can use the bot in your DingTalk enterprise. You can chat with the bot privately, or add the bot to a group and `@mention the bot` to trigger a conversation.

![Image 8](/imgs/dingtalk-bot-8.png)


## FAQ

### How to start a new chat history

If you want to reset your chat history, you can send a `Reset` message to the bot (case-sensitive), and the bot will start a new chat history.
